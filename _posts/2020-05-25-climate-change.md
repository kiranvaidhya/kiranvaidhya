# It's climate change we should be worried about, not AI

The 2019-2020 transition was remarkable for the amount of chaos we witnessed. We saw the Australian fires, a passenger plane being shot down by Iranian military and the beginning of the deadly COVID-19.

This has lead to a major perspective shift in my head. It's not AI that we should be worried about, it's climate change. If anything, the COVID-19 pandemic brought every single nation to its knees by forcing them to impose draconian lockdowns never seen before in history. It also highlighted several deficiences in the systems that enable multiple nations to work together, through international bodies like United Nations and World Health Organization.

Combined with the criticisms deep learning has been facing, it's hard to imagine how AI systems can evolve so much in a short span of time before climate change lands a deadly blow on the already fragile international collaboration.
