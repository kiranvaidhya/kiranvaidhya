Research

Lung

Diagnostic Image Analysis Group, Radboudumc

Lung cancer screening is being rolled out worldwide. Aberle et al reiterate the efficacy of screening high risk population with low-dose CT scans for lung cancer. My research interests revolve around early lung cancer detection on low-dose chest CT scans with an emphasis on temporal analysis using medical image analysis techniques.
